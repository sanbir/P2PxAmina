Bring your assets onto the ledger — once

Triora is a ledger over your own custody. You tokenize what you bring into 1:1 accounting tokens (cBTC, cETH, cUSDC); real assets stay put under AMINA tri-party and move only once, at settlement. Tokenize collateral to borrow, tokenize USDC to lend.

Tokenize collateral — to borrow

cBTC · cETH · cSOL

Lock BTC, ETH or SOL once and borrow against it many times. Your asset never leaves custody — the protocol works with a 1:1 token.

1
Connect address & read balance
Connect a custody vault or Safe — we read the on-chain balance. Nothing moves.
2
Add AMINA as tri-party
AMINA takes control of the address via a control agreement (or as a co-signer on your Safe).
3
Chainlink mints cBTC / cETH 1:1
Now it's reusable collateral. Go to Markets and Borrow USDC against it.

Tokenize USDC — to lend

cUSDC

To lend, first tokenize the USDC you want to deploy into cUSDC. Your real USDC stays in your custody and moves only once, at settlement.

1
Connect USDC custody & read balance
Connect Binance / Anchorage or a Safe — we read your USDC balance.
2
Add AMINA as tri-party
AMINA goes tri-party over the USDC address so it can co-sign the single settlement transfer out.
3
Chainlink mints cUSDC 1:1
cUSDC is your reserved liquidity, available to lend. Go to Markets and Lend.
